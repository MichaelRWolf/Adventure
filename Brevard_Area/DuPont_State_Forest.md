# DuPont State Recreational Forest

10,000+ acres spanning Henderson and Transylvania counties, ~25 miles from Lake Toxaway home base. 80 miles of trails for hiking, biking, and horses. Famous for its waterfalls — Triple Falls was a filming location for *The Hunger Games*.

## Swimming

- **Hooker Falls**: Best swimming in DuPont. 12-ft drop, 75 ft wide, calm pool on the Little River. Easy 0.4-mile walk from the Buck Forest Rd / Hooker Falls trailhead. Very popular in summer. No lifeguard.
- **Fawn Lake**: Swimming allowed. One of the few lake swimming options in the area.

### Rules
- No jumping, sliding, or diving from waterfalls.
- No swimming or wading within 300 ft upstream of any waterfall.
- Swim at your own risk — no lifeguards.

## Waterfall Hikes

| Waterfall | Notes |
|-----------|-------|
| Triple Falls | *Hunger Games* filming site. 3-tier, ~120 ft total. Most photographed in DuPont. |
| High Falls | Tallest in DuPont (~150 ft). Viewing platform; less accessible pool. |
| Bridal Veil Falls | Broad, curtain-style fall. Part of the Triple/High/Bridal Veil loop. |
| Hooker Falls | Lowest and widest; best for swimming. Often done as add-on to the main loop. |

The 2.2-mile Triple/High/Bridal Veil loop is the standard route; add Hooker Falls (~0.8 mi extra) for a full day.
