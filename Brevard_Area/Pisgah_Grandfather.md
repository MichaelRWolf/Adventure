# Pisgah National Forest — Grandfather Ranger District

Headquarters in Marion, NC. Covers the area around Grandfather Mountain, the Blue Ridge foothills, and the Wilson Creek Wild and Scenic River corridor. More remote than the Pisgah Ranger District.

## Swimming

- **Wilson Creek**: Wild and Scenic River with multiple swimming holes. Access via NC-181 south of Grandfather Mountain. Cold, clear water; rocky pools.

## Dispersed Camping

Dispersed camping permitted throughout. Wilson Creek corridor is popular for multi-night trips.
