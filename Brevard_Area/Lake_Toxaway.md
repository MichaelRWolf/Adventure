# Lake Toxaway, NC

Brevard, NC bills itself as the **Land of Waterfalls** — Transylvania County alone has over 250 named falls and 1,000+ miles of trails winding through Pisgah National Forest, DuPont State Recreational Forest, and Gorges State Park.

## Home Base

**Horseplay Lane, Lake Toxaway, NC 28747**
Coordinates: 35.1313°N, 82.8893°W

Private lane off US-64 (Rosman Hwy), in the Quebec community neighborhood.
Nearest landmarks: T.C. Henderson Elementary School and Quebec Community Center (both on Rosman Hwy, essentially at the lane entrance).

## Swimming

- **Lake Toxaway** (main lake): Private — property owners, guests, and Greystone Inn guests only. No public access.
- **Gorges State Park rivers/creeks**: Swimming prohibited due to swift currents.
- **Hidden Falls (Horsepasture River)**: Best public swimming option. Family-friendly pool below a small (<10 ft) waterfall in Pisgah National Forest, just downstream from Rainbow Falls. Often missed by hikers — it's a few steps off the main trail.

## Waterfall Hikes

| Waterfall | Distance from Home Base | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Toxaway Falls | 7 miles | Right on US-64 at the dam. Roadside pull-off, walkway at top. 150-ft drop. Dog-friendly, easy 0.6-mi hike. |
| Raven Rock Falls | ~10 miles | NC-281 N → Cold Mountain Rd. Short hike (~10 min), 400-450 ft drop. On private property (Lake Toxaway Estates) — no trespassing signs may appear. |
| Rainbow Falls | ~15 miles | Gorges State Park, Horsepasture River. 150-ft drop with rainbow-mist effect. Moderate difficulty. |
| Hidden Falls | ~15 miles | Same trail as Rainbow Falls. Small fall, great swimming pool. Often missed. |
| Turtleback Falls | ~15 miles | Same Horsepasture River corridor as Rainbow/Hidden Falls. |
| Whitewater Falls | ~20 miles | 400+ ft drop — highest waterfall east of the Rocky Mountains. Paved walk, wheelchair accessible. |
| DuPont State Forest (Triple/High/Bridal Veil Falls) | ~25 miles | 2.2-mile loop connecting three major falls. Well-maintained, popular. |
| Schoolhouse Falls | ~25 miles | Near Cold Mountain trailhead in Panthertown Valley, nice pool at base. |

## Regional Context

- **Gorges State Park**: Immediately south of Lake Toxaway. Rugged gorges, temperate rainforest, rare plant species. Fishing, camping, hiking, mountain biking — but no swimming.
- **Pisgah National Forest**: Surrounds the area; Rainbow Falls and Hidden Falls are within it.
- **DuPont State Recreational Forest**: ~25 miles northeast; featured in *The Hunger Games* (Triple Falls).
- **Nantahala National Forest**: To the west.
