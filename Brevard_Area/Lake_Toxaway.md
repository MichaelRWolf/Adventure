# Lake Toxaway, NC

Brevard, NC bills itself as the **Land of Waterfalls** — Transylvania County alone has over 250 named falls and 1,000+ miles of trails winding through Pisgah National Forest, DuPont State Recreational Forest, and Gorges State Park.

## No Swimming

- **Lake Toxaway** (main lake): Private — property owners, guests, and Greystone Inn guests only. No public access.
- **Gorges State Park rivers/creeks**: Swimming prohibited due to swift currents.

## Swimming

- **Hidden Falls** (drive 15 mi, 2 mi RT): Family-friendly pool below small (<10 ft) fall on Horsepasture River in Pisgah NF; just off Rainbow Falls trail, often missed.
- **Turtleback Falls** (drive 15 mi, 1.5 mi RT): Natural waterslide and swim hole; same Horsepasture corridor as Hidden Falls.
- **Schoolhouse Falls** (drive 25 mi, 2.5 mi RT): Deep tannin plunge pool; can walk behind falls. See Panthertown Valley section.
- **Granny Burrell Falls** (drive 25 mi, 4 mi RT): Beach-style pool; downstream add-on from Schoolhouse. See Panthertown Valley section.
- **Hooker Falls — DuPont** (drive 25 mi, 0.6 mi RT): Calm, deep pool; one of the best in the area.
- **Champion Park Pool, Rosman** (drive 10 mi): Public outdoor pool; closes 5 PM (verify hours — may vary by season).
- **French Broad River, Rosman** (drive 10 mi): Informal river swimming off Champion Park; generally safe but check [Swim Guide](https://theswimguide.org) after rain.
- **Avery Creek — Pisgah Ranger District** (drive 20 mi): Multiple pools along Avery Creek Trail; Pisgah Stables trailhead off FR 477. See [[Pisgah_Ranger]].

## Panthertown Valley

6,700-acre backcountry in Nantahala NF; 25 mi from home base. Often called "Yosemite of the East."

| Attraction           | Drive (~mi) | Hike RT (~mi) | Swim             | Notes                                      |
|----------------------|-------------|---------------|------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| Schoolhouse Falls    | 25          | 2.5           | Permitted/Family | 20–25 ft cascade; tannin pool; walk behind |
| Granny Burrell Falls | 25          | 4             | Permitted/Family | Beach-style pool; Panthertown Creek        |

- **Trailhead:** US-64 W → NC-281 N → Cold Mountain Rd 5.75 mi → Panthertown/Cold Mountain Gap Trailhead
- **Schoolhouse Falls:** Easy-to-moderate; large deep plunge pool; can walk behind the falls
- **Granny Burrell Falls:** Continue down Panthertown Creek past Schoolhouse; wider, beach-like area
