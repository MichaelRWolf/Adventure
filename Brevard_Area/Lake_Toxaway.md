# Lake Toxaway, NC

Brevard, NC bills itself as the **Land of Waterfalls** — Transylvania County alone has over 250 named falls and 1,000+ miles of trails winding through Pisgah National Forest, DuPont State Recreational Forest, and Gorges State Park.

## No Swimming

- **Lake Toxaway** (main lake): Private — property owners, guests, and Greystone Inn guests only. No public access.
- **Gorges State Park rivers/creeks**: Swimming prohibited due to swift currents.

## Swimming

- **Hidden Falls** (~15 mi): Best nearby public option. Family-friendly pool below a small (<10 ft) waterfall on the Horsepasture River in Pisgah National Forest, just downstream from Rainbow Falls. Often missed — a few steps off the main trail.
- **Turtleback Falls** (~15 mi): Same Horsepasture River corridor as Hidden Falls. Natural waterslide and swimming hole.
- **Schoolhouse Falls** (~25 mi): Nice pool at the base, near Cold Mountain trailhead in Panthertown Valley.
- **Hooker Falls — DuPont** (~25 mi): Short, wide falls (10–15 ft) with a calm, deep pool. One of the best swimming holes in the area. Easy 0.3-mile walk from Buck Forest Rd trailhead. Very popular in summer.
- **Avery Creek — Pisgah Ranger District** (~20 mi): Clear, cold mountain creek with multiple pools along the Avery Creek Trail. Park at Pisgah Stables trailhead off FR 477. See [[Pisgah_Ranger]] for details and dispersed camping.
